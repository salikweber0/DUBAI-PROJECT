Dubai Overview

Dubai is the most populous city in the United Arab Emirates and the capital of the Emirate of Dubai. Located on the southeast coast of the Persian Gulf, Dubai has transformed from a small fishing village into a global metropolis in just a few decades.


Known for its ultramodern architecture, luxury shopping, vibrant nightlife, and year-round sunshine, Dubai has become a major global city and business hub. The city is famous for its ambitious projects, innovative architecture, and world-class tourism facilities.


What makes Dubai truly special is its perfect blend of traditional Arab culture with modern international influences, creating a unique multicultural environment.

History & Development

1833
Dubai was established as a fishing settlement by the Al Maktoum family, who continue to rule the emirate today.
1960s
Discovery of oil transformed Dubai's economy, but visionary leaders invested in infrastructure and diversification.
1985
Emirates airline was founded, establishing Dubai as a major aviation hub connecting East and West.
2010
Burj Khalifa opened, making Dubai home to the world's tallest building and a global architectural icon.
2020
Dubai Expo 2020 showcased the city's innovation and hospitality to the world, attracting millions of visitors.

My Personal Connection

Dubai represents everything I admire about human ambition and achievement. The first time I learned about this incredible city, I was amazed by how it transformed from a desert into a global metropolis in just a few decades.


Dubai doesn't just build buildings; it builds dreams and makes the impossible possible.

What fascinates me most about Dubai is its commitment to excellence in everything - from world-class infrastructure to exceptional hospitality. The city's vision of becoming the best destination in the world inspires me to dream big and work hard toward my goals.


Dubai's multicultural environment, where people from over 200 countries live and work together harmoniously, shows that diversity is truly a strength. This city has taught me that with vision, determination, and hard work, anything is achievable.
